Hi! I'm Aniket Handa, a Software Engineer conditioned with Interaction Design skills. I fortunately find myself in a magical intersection where bits meet humans. I pride myself in crafting user experiences that bridge the gap between what’s possible (using technology) and what’s desirable (by people). I recently graduated with a Masters degree in Human-Computer Interaction + Design (MHCID) from University of Washington, Seattle. Before joining this multidisciplinary program I earned a dual Masters and Bachelors degrees in Computer Science & Engineering from Jaypee Institute of Information Technology, Noida. I get excited when I hear HCI, Wearables, UbiComp, Ambient Intelligent devices, Computer Vision, Data Visualization.
