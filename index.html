Hi! I'm Aniket Handa, a Software Engineer and a budding Interaction Designer. I fortunately find myself in a magical intersection where bits meet humans. I pride myself in crafting user experiences that bridge the gap between what’s possible (using technology) and what’s desirable (by people). Currently I'm a graduate student in Human-Computer Interaction + Design (MHCID) at University of Washington, Seattle. Before joining this multidisciplinary program I earned a dual Masters and Bachelors degrees in Computer Science & Engineering from Jaypee Institute of Information Technology, Noida. My interest lies around HCI, Wearables, UbiComp, Ambient Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Data Visualization.
